HOW TO PLAY

Controls

  • Drag the character back on the slingshot
  • Release to launch
  • Click or press SPACE during flight for special ability

Objective

  • Destroy all the pigs to complete the level
  • Use fewer birds for a higher score
  • Glass breaks easily, wood takes a few hits, stone is tough

Tips

  • Aim for weak points in the structure
  • Toppling blocks onto pigs crushes them
  • Unused birds give bonus points
